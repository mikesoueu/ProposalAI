Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, request on-demand services, contact customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include: name, email, phone number, postal address, profile picture, payment method, and other information you choose to provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect about you to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services;
  • Process payments and send related information (via our partner, Stripe);
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages;
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this privacy policy (e.g., sharing with payment processors like Stripe to facilitate transactions).

4. AI Generation

The content you input for proposal generation is sent securely to our AI providers (e.g., OpenRouter, OpenAI, Anthropic) strictly for the purpose of generating your document. We do not use your private proposals to train our own models.
