About Us

We're on a mission to help freelancers and agencies close deals faster.

Our Story

ProposalAI was born out of a simple frustration: writing proposals takes too much time. As freelancers and agency owners ourselves, we spent countless hours drafting, formatting, and chasing clients for signatures and payments.

We realized that while the world moved fast, the way we pitched clients was stuck in the past. We needed a tool that could instantly translate a rough idea into a beautiful, persuasive, and legally sound proposal.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower independent professionals and agencies to compete with the big players. By automating the tedious parts of the sales process—drafting, designing, and collecting payments—we free you up to do what you do best: deliver amazing work.
